# PulseLink Proof of Concept (POC)

## Objective

To demonstrate that PulseLink can successfully connect a patient requiring blood with a compatible donor.

## Scope

The POC will validate the core workflow of the platform without advanced features such as AI recommendations or hospital integration.

## Workflow

### Step 1: Donor Registration

A donor registers with:

* Name
* Phone Number
* Address
* Blood Group
* Last Donation Date

### Step 2: Blood Request Creation

A patient creates a blood request with:

* Patient Name
* Phone Number
* Hospital Address
* Required Blood Group

### Step 3: Donor Matching

The system:

* Finds matching blood groups
* Checks donor availability
* Verifies donation eligibility

### Step 4: Notification

The system notifies matching donors.

### Step 5: Donor Response

The donor accepts or rejects the request.

### Step 6: Contact Sharing

If accepted:

* Patient receives donor details.
* Request status changes to Accepted.

## Success Criteria

* Donor registration works successfully.
* Blood requests are created successfully.
* Matching donors are identified correctly.
* Donor responses are recorded.
* Patient receives donor information after acceptance.

## Expected Outcome

A complete end-to-end workflow proving that PulseLink can reduce the time required to connect patients with compatible blood donors during emergencies.
